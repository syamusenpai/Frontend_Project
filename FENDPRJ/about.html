About Smart Grocery

Making Grocery Shopping
Smarter & Healthier

Smart Grocery Price & Nutrition Checker helps users compare grocery prices, view nutrition information, and make healthier shopping decisions through an easy-to-use web application.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make grocery shopping easier, healthier, and more affordable. By providing price comparison and nutrition information in one platform, users can make informed decisions before purchasing everyday groceries.

We also aim to provide an accessible website that supports different users, including children, adults, and colour-blind users.
